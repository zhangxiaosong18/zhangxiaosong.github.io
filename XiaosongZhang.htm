Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate of PRISDL in the School of Electronic, Electrical and Communication Engineering, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences , advised by Prof. Qixiang Ye. I got a B.S. degree in Electronic Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology, Weihai in June 2018.

My research interests include computer vision and machine learning, specifically for visual object detection and feature learning.
